GoalGlow ✨ Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

GoalGlow is an allowance-budgeting app designed for kids aged 10–16, set up together with a parent or guardian. It is built so that we cannot see your data — because it never leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. GoalGlow has no accounts, no sign-up, no server and no analytics. We do not collect, transmit, store, sell or share any personal information — from children or adults.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — names, allowance amounts, spending entries, savings goals and goal photos — is stored only in your browser or device's local storage on the device you use it on. It is never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app or clearing your browser data permanently removes it.

Photos

When you add a photo of your savings goal, it is saved on your device only. GoalGlow never uploads photos.

Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

GoalGlow is designed for children with parental involvement: setup and settings live behind a PIN-protected Parent Mode. Because no data is collected or transmitted, no personal information about children is ever processed by us or by third parties.

Ads and third parties

GoalGlow contains no advertising, no in-app purchases and no third-party trackers.

No real money

GoalGlow is a tracking and learning tool. It does not connect to bank accounts and cannot move real money.
